About Me

I am pursuing second year Bachelor's degree in mass media, during my second year of studying mass media, I've fallen in love with making shortfilms. I enjoy coming up with interesting stories and learning how to make them into cool videos. Short films are my favorite because I can tell a powerful story in a short amount of time. I like learning about how to film scenes, edit videos, and write scripts. I also like working with other students and professionals to learn more about the film industry. I love talking to people and sharing ideas to make the world of media more exciting. For me communication is the medium to get connected with the people around you.
